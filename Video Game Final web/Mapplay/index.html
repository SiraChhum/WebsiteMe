Taipei

Overview Taipei is a breathtaking new map in your MMORPG, blending fantasy elements with stunning natural beauty and mystical energy. This vast, open-world region features towering mountains, enchanted forests, and ancient ruins, all infused with magical energy. Players will explore legendary temples, hidden valleys, and sky-high peaks filled with both wonder and danger.

Key Locations & Unique Features

Celestial Peak (Mountain Summit & Floating Isles) :

At the highest point of Taipei, Celestial Peak is a series of floating islands connected by ancient stone bridges and glowing energy paths. The air is filled with swirling mist, and mythical phoenixes soar between the peaks. Players who reach the summit will find the Shrine of the Sky Monarch, where rare quests and legendary artifacts can be obtained.

Moonlit Blossom Valley (Enchanted Cherry Blossom Forest) :

A magical valley covered in glowing cherry blossoms that bloom year-round. The petals float in the air, and the ground shimmers with soft blue light at night. Spirits of the past wander here, offering cryptic clues to hidden treasures. However, powerful nature guardians ensure that only the worthy may pass through safely.

Jade Serpent River (Mystical Waterway & Secret Caves) :

A radiant jade-green river winds through the map, leading players through breathtaking waterfalls and mysterious caverns. Some caves hide ancient Dragon Spirit Seals, which must be unlocked to awaken the legendary Emerald Serpent, a world boss that grants powerful rewards.
